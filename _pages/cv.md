Projects

* __Stanford RNA 3D Folding__: [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/stanford-rna-3d-folding/writeups/state-of-the-rnart)
  * Independently investigated state-of-the-art methods for RNA tertiary structure prediction by benchmarking and running inference with multiple CASP16-derived frameworks, including Protenix (AF3), DRFold2, NuFold, RhoFold+, DeepFoldRNA, and trRosettaRNA. Conducted an extensive literature and model survey spanning ab initio, template-based, and deep learning approaches, and designed a multi-model ensemble integrating inference and refinement pipelines for improved generalization. Explored fine-tuning of AlphaFold3-based models on newly released PDB structures and implemented sequence-chunking strategies for handling long RNAs under limited GPU resources. The project deepened my understanding of biomolecular folding, CASP methodology, and cross-domain applications of AI in structural biology.

This chatbot delivers precise, context-aware responses, tailored to the unique needs of Persian-speaking users.
