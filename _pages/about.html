I am an MD candidate at Tehran University of Medical Sciences (expected 2027) and a machine learning engineer specializing in medical imaging and vision-language models. My work sits at the intersection of clinical medicine and deep learning.

My long-term research direction is digital biology: training models that understand cellular behavior, structural biology, and disease from multimodal data. I am particularly inspired by the vision of simulating life at the molecular and cellular level and by what AI systems like AlphaFold have already demonstrated is possible.

Currently I contribute and compete on Kaggle (Expert) and take on applied ML contracts to sharpen execution alongside theory.
