# About me
I am a medical student fascinated by the mind-bending complexity of life and its ultimate masterpiece: intelligence. With a background in biology and machine learning, I’m on an exciting journey to explore how intelligence, whether it’s born from nature or crafted by humans, emerges, adapts, and evolves. My research interests span applications of AI in computational biology, medical imaging, and complex systems. I like games and enjoy teaching machines to learn about the world and ultimate game of life.
